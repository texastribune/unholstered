About this project

The Texas Tribune spent almost a year investigating police shootings in Texas’ largest cities. We worked to obtain official records — primarily through open records requests — from police departments in the state’s 36 cities with a population of 100,000 or more residents. We worked to independently confirm details related to those shootings, and pored over hundreds of incident reports. The Tribune’s Unholstered project offers the most detailed look at the state of police shootings in Texas.
